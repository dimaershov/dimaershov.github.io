1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following categories of information:

  • Advertising data, such as ad load events, impressions, clicks, rewarded ad completion, and interstitial or banner ad events.

We use information to provide and improve gameplay features, save progress and settings, show ads and rewarded ads, measure app performance, understand how the App is used, detect errors, respond to support requests, deliver or restore purchases, prevent fraud, and comply with legal, platform, and App Store requirements.

The App may show ads, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. The App uses advertising partners such as Yandex Mobile Ads and may include mediation partners through that SDK.

Where required by Apple, the App asks for permission through App Tracking Transparency before using data to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. You can allow or deny tracking in iOS settings. If you deny tracking, ads may still be shown, but they may be less relevant.

4. Analytics

The App uses analytics services such as Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, improve levels and features, and diagnose issues. Analytics events may include gameplay actions, language, level identifiers, progress percentages, coin balance changes, purchase events, ad events, and technical metadata.

Virtual coins and similar in-app items have no cash value, are not transferable, and may only be used inside the App. Coins may be earned through gameplay, rewarded ads, promotions, or purchased through in-app purchases where available.

Purchase history and transaction status may be used to deliver purchased items, restore purchases, prevent fraud, provide support, and understand purchase performance.
